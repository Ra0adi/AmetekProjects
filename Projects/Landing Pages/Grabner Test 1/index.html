How to Prevent Vapor Lock in Ethanol Blending

With the surge in Ethanol usage in gasoline by petroleum refineries, coupled with the extended periods of scorching weather worldwide, there is a burgeoning interest in conducting fuel tests to mitigate the risk of vapor lock.

In the United States, an increasing number of states are embracing the E10 rule, which authorizes the addition of 10% Ethanol (EtOH) to the fuel mix.
